To place an album simply click on the album art for each category.
Dragging an image from one category to another will swap the recordings.
To save the chart as an image, simply right-click an area outside the album art and select "Save image as...".
To save your data, press "Show Options" and then "Export". You can later use the exported file to load your data by pasting its contents in the "Import" box.
The "Show Options" button offers some rudimentary integration with RateYourMusic to help you find your favorite albums from each category.
You can also customize the chart by checking out the options offered by the "Show Options" button.
